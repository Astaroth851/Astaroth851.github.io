About Ryan Bates - Production Sound Recordist


Hi, I'm Ryan Bates, a Location Sound Recordist for TV, Film, & Commercial Video Production. I’m based in Little Rock and available for travel shoots. Ultimately my job as a Sound Recordist is all about capturing voices; Whether that’s a documentary interview, speaker on stage, a commercial, or actors in a narrative film, if a production needs dialog recorded I'm there.

Before specializing in recording sound for video, I studied film at the University of Central Arkansas. I've always enjoyed the idea of working on team based projects as a specialized role. Seeing how technical and important audio is for film projects, I developed a passion for it. A film can have stunning visuals, but if the audio is subpar it throws the audience off and can be distracting. That's why I decided to take on the role as an audio technician. I want the audience to be able to focus on the content they are watching; I want everybody's voice to be heard loud and clear. As soon as I graduated from college I invested a lot of money on audio equipment and have been working on various projects ever since.


When you hire me, you can expect quality through experience coupled with an extensive knowledge base & top tier equipment. I love unique projects and am equipped to handle projects of any scale. Let’s discuss the unique needs of your production today!
